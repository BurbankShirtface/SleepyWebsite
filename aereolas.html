The Aereolas

Bored at the Top of Out Lungs

Released May 1, 2009

The Aereolas were a short lived, five-piece group that appeared around 2008/2009. They released one album that was written and mostly recorded in the span of about two weeks, which is fairly evident. It was mostly recorded at a cottage in Amberley, Ontario, with the exception of some vocals, which were recorded in Coco's basement on Sarnia St. in Bayfield, Ontario.
