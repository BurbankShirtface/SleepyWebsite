Sleepy Punk Recordings

Sleepy Punk Recordings is a kind of record label that mostly acts as a storage container for songs made by a group of people who do what they like. It started around 2005 and continues to grow and evolve. Although the headquarters has moved and doesn't even really physically exist, we are happy to say we were founded in and operating out of Bayfield, Ontario.
